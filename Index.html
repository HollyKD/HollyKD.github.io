Driving Test Cancellation Checker

How it works

Getting a driving test that isn't six months away is painstaking, so let's take the effort out of it and let your device let you know when there is a new test!

All you have to do is go to the test checker via the Check Now button at the top of this page or click here. Once on the checker put the name of your centre in. When there is a cancellation, a noise will be made so long as the window remains open. Once you hear the noise, log in to the DVSA website as normal to decide if you want the test.

Happy hunting!

Completely free, with no hidden charges!
