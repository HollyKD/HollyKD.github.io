How to Book a Driving Test

Booking a First Test

  1. Go to the DVSA website booking facility
  2. Enter your driving license number
  3. Choose an available date
  4. Pay the fee and fill in your details
  5. Receive email confirmation

Booking a Cancellation

  1. Go to the DVSA website change booking facility
  2. Enter your driving license number and booking reference
  3. Find a date that suits you and confirm, this can be done up to 6 times before cancellation is necessary to change and fee is returned any time so long as it is not within the 3 working day notice period required by the DVSA
